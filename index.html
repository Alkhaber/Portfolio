About me

Designer who codes.
Developer who designs.

I combine human-centered design thinking with hands-on development — from Figma prototypes to working Android apps and hardware concepts.

I hold a Bachelor's in Informatics: Design, Use & Interaction from the University of Oslo, and I'm currently pursuing a Master's in Entrepreneurship and Innovation Management at UiO — building at the intersection of design, technology, and business.

During my studies, I've worked across the full spectrum: conducting user research and interviews, building interactive Figma prototypes, writing code in Kotlin, C++, Python and React, and recently taking on hardware product design and startup analysis in a team setting.

I thrive in collaborative teams, bring a structured approach to problem-solving, and am passionate about creating products that genuinely improve people's lives.
